# Global Components

## Header

- Logo
- Primary navigation
- Request Consultation button

## Footer

- Business name, tagline, and logo
- Credentials (SAFE, IADE, CQDE)
- Services summary (shortened list, linking to the Services page)
- Phone number
- Email address
- Office/location information
- Quick navigation links
- Copyright

## Call-to-Action

A consistent "Request a Consultation" call-to-action should appear throughout the website.

---

# Page Purposes

## Home

Introduce the business, establish credibility, and guide visitors toward requesting a consultation.

## About

Provide Ashley's background, education, experience, and professional qualifications.

## Services

Explain the services offered and help visitors determine whether Precision Handwriting Analysis can assist with their needs.

## Kentucky

Dedicated landing page focused on Kentucky clients and search traffic.

## Ohio

Dedicated landing page focused on Ohio clients and search traffic.

## FAQ

Answer common questions about forensic document examination and the consultation process.

## Contact

Provide visitors with clear ways to request a consultation.
