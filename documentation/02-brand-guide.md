# Precision Handwriting Analysis Brand Guide

## Brand Identity

**Business Name:** Precision Handwriting Analysis, LLC

**Brand Style:** Modern, professional, trustworthy, and approachable.

The website should communicate expertise and credibility while remaining clean, elegant, and easy to navigate.

## Design Direction

The website should feel:

- Clean
- Modern
- Elegant
- Professional
- Easy to read

Avoid:

- Busy layouts
- Excessive animations
- Bright colors
- Generic stock imagery
- Overly corporate styling

---

## Photography Style

Use:

- Ashley's professional headshots
- Handwritten documents
- Pens
- Paper textures
- Professional office imagery

Avoid:

- Crime scene imagery
- Fingerprints
- Police-related graphics
- Generic business meetings

---

## Tone of Voice

The website copy should be:

- Professional
- Confident
- Informative
- Objective
- Welcoming

Avoid overly promotional language.

---

## Design Inspiration

Client reference websites emphasize:

- Professional credibility
- Clean layouts
- Strong typography
- Professional headshots
- Qualifications and experience

Final design direction:

A modern, professional website that reflects the precision, credibility, and expertise expected of a forensic document examiner. The design should be clean, approachable, and easy to navigate while inspiring confidence in potential clients.
